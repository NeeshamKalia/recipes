Best Punjabi food Recipes

Paneer Tikka

Paneer tikka is a delicious must-have vegetarian snack at any party. A tikka recipe that will surely give you the restaurant-style paneer tikka that you've been craving for. It is a deligh for all the vegetarian food lovers.

Punjabi Dal Tadka

This Punjabi dal tadka is a mouth-watering dish served with roti, naan or rice. Any north Indian menu is incomplete without this tadka dal. Almost every Indian restaurant has the delicious dal tadka on their menu as the most popular dish.
